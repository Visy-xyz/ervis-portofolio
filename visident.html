About VisiDent

VisiDent is a modern dental service platform that offers a seamless experience for accessing dental services and consultations.

  • Appointment Booking: Schedule dental appointments with real-time availability.
  • Service Information: Explore various dental services from cosmetic dentistry to orthodontics.
  • Online Consultation: Chat with licensed dentists directly through the platform.
  • Dental Care Tips: Read articles and watch videos on oral hygiene and dental wellness.

Technologies Used

  • HTML: Structuring content.
  • CSS: Styling and layout.
  • JavaScript: Interactivity and dynamic content.
  • Bootstrap: Responsive and fast development.
